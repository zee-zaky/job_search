# BA CV Guide

## Suitable Titles

- Senior Business Analyst
- Senior Technical Business Analyst
- Business Analyst / Technical Delivery Consultant
- Product-focused Senior Business Analyst

## Summary Emphasis

Emphasise discovery, design thinking, stakeholder facilitation, requirements analysis, current/future state process mapping, user stories, acceptance criteria, technical analysis, data validation, integration/API understanding, Agile delivery support, and cross-functional delivery.

## Capability Groups

- Human-Centred Discovery & Design Thinking
- Stakeholder Engagement & Facilitation
- Requirements, Process & Service Analysis
- Technical Business Analysis & Data Validation

## Strong Evidence Sources

- Westpac CRM/Dynamics 365/KYC/CCCFA/AML analysis.
- MSD legislative and operational change initiatives.
- NSW RMS digital government transformation.
- Alinma IT advisory, business analysis and enterprise platform work.
- TWG integration architecture if the BA role has strong technical/platform content.
