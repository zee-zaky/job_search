# AI CV Guide

## Suitable Titles

- Senior Business Analyst with Applied AI Capability
- AI Business Analyst
- Applied AI and Automation Business Analyst
- Senior Technical Business Analyst

## Summary Emphasis

Emphasise the combination of senior BA experience and recent Master of Artificial Intelligence work. Highlight applied AI, NLP, RAG, AI agents, metadata extraction, model evaluation, Python experimentation, data-driven decision-making, and business-to-technical translation.

## Capability Groups

- Applied AI, RAG & Agentic Workflows
- Human-Centred Discovery & Automation Opportunity Analysis
- Data Analysis, Validation & Model Evaluation
- Stakeholder Engagement & Technical Business Analysis

## Strong Evidence Sources

- Legal case retrieval using AI-generated metadata.
- COLIEE-2026 sparse retrieval and legal reasoning metadata work.
- AI agents, RAG, prompt engineering, context engineering and tool-use workflows.
- CaringCompanion robot and TurtleBot4 autonomous navigation.
- Transformer-based NLP model fine-tuning and evaluation.
- BA experience translating business needs into implementable requirements.
